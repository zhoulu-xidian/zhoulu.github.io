Lu Zhou （周路）

Associate Professor, Ph.D. Supervisor

I am currently an Associate Professor in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Xidian University. My research interests include IoT system security and privacy.

Education

  • Ph.D., Computer Science and technology, 2015.09 - 2022.03
    Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China
    Advisor: Prof. Haojin Zhu
    Area of Study: IoT Security and Privacy

  • B.S., Computer Science and technology, 2011.09 - 2015.06
    Sichuan Universiry, Chengdu, China

Research

Up to 06/2026, I published top-tier papers including USENIX Security (2023), ACM CCS (2024), IEEE INFOCOM (2025), WWW (2025, 2026a, 2026b), ACM FSE (2026), ACM UIST (2025), IEEE TDSC (2025a, 2025b), IEEE TON (2025a, 2025b), and IEEE TIFS (2019).
